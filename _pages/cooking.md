## Whole-meal Pita bread pizza

Ingredients:

1. 1 slice of whole-meal pita bread
2. 1 slice of edam cheese
3. Oregano (optional)

Instructions:

Put the cheese on the pita bread. Optional oregano too.
Then 30 seconds to 1 minute in the microwave.
