My tsundoku list

Normally a cheap-bastard, but never save money on books. Except when I can buy the paperback instead of the hardcover book. Whenever I go to a new place, I always check the bookstores and second-hand stores. If not for something that I am already looking for (like Arto Paasilinna in English or Barry Crump's Bastards I have met), then for recommendations for a new book or series.
