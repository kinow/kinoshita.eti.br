Books I have already read

The are some books about computers (programming, networks, operating systems, etc) and administration/management (strategic planning, basic administration, etc) due to my graduation in computers and administration.

In 2007 I went to New Zealand for the first time. That was my first time overseas as well. After that I decided to enhance my English, so started having private classes with David Rotenberg. He told me that reading was good to learn another language, and lent me One for The Money.

I still keep the book, but started reading other novels, short stories, mangas, dictionaries, and other stuff. Not everything is listed here. Thanks David!
