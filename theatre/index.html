These are some plays I watched. Feel free to suggest me some good plays that may be missing from my list!

  • Bartleby
    • March, 2008
    • Teatro SESC Paulista, São Paulo, Brazil
    • http://guia.folha.uol.com.br/teatro/ult10053u375791.shtml
    • A adaptação do conto clássico do escritor Herman Melville, “Bartleby”, estréia na unidade provisória do Sesc Avenida Paulista na próxima sexta-feira (29), às 20h. A montagem foi feita pelo núcleo Caixa Preta, da Cooperativa Paulista de Teatro, a direção é de Joaquim Goulart e o elenco é composto por Cácia Goulart e Rodrigo Gaion. “Bartleby” conta a história de um jovem escriturário judicial que, cansado do trabalho burocrático, adota o “não” como lema e o “nada” como estilo de vida, consagrando o imobilismo. A montagem é uma crítica sobre os padrões do mundo moderno

  • The Mooncake and The Kumara
    • 30 June, 2017
    • Q Theatre, Auckland, New Zealand
    • https://www.eventfinda.co.nz/2017/the-mooncake-and-the-kumara/auckland
    • Nearly ninety years ago on a New Zealand market garden, two families, one Māori and the other Chinese, became part of a romance that would uproot their lives over generations. Layered with myth and fable, The Mooncake and the Kūmara is a moving story about a mixed-up, Māori-Chinese love affair that sprouts among rows of potatoes. Told in a rich mixture of English, Māori and Cantonese, The Mooncake and the Kūmara is the debut, award-winning play by Māori-Chinese playwright Mei-Lin Te Puea Hansen.
  • Blonde Poison
    • August, 2017
    • Herald Theatre, Auckland, New Zealand
    • http://www.nzherald.co.nz/entertainment/news/article.cfm?c_id=1501119&objectid=11910069
    • Inspired by the true story of Stella Goldschlag, Blonde Poison tells the haunting story of a Jewish woman living illegally in war-torn Berlin. Betrayed and tortured, she agrees to become a Greifer - a Gestapo informer - in exchange for her life and the lives of her beloved family. Decades later, she receives an unexpected phone call from a journalist that changes everything.
